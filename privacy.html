Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Avenue (“Avenue”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) handles personal information when you use the Avenue mobile application, website, and related services.

This page is an initial policy for the Avenue MVP and should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before public launch.

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you use Avenue, we may collect:

  • Account information, such as your email address and account identifier.
  • Content you submit, including event or venue information.
  • Saved events, followed venues, and other app preferences.
  • Approximate or precise location, only when you give device permission, to show relevant nearby results.
  • Technical and usage information needed to operate, secure, and improve the service.

2. How we use information

We use information to provide and personalise Avenue, authenticate users, display nearby events and venues, process submissions, maintain security, respond to support requests, and improve the service.

3. Legal bases

Where applicable under data protection law, we process information to perform our agreement with you, based on your consent, to meet legal obligations, and for legitimate interests such as operating and securing Avenue.

4. How information is shared

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Avenue, such as hosting, database, mapping, analytics, and email providers. Public event and venue submissions may be visible to other users after approval. We do not sell personal information.

5. Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request account deletion at any time.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or receive a copy of your personal information, and to withdraw consent. You can also control location access in your device settings.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

Avenue is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International processing

Our providers may process information in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Avenue develops. We will post the new version here and update the date above.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email privacy@avenuecy.com.
